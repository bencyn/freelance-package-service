    Seller Tips

    1. Be original. What’s different about what you have to offer?
    2. Be as specific as you can about the service you are offering and exactly what the Buyer will receive if they purchase your Service.
    3. Set a realistic delivery timeframe. Late delivery will lead to unhappy Buyers and will affect your rankings.
    4. Make your Service standout with great quality images or even video illustrating the service that you are offering.

    Don't Do

    1. Include your full name, company name or contact details anywhere in the Service description or title.
    2. Direct Buyers to another website for full details of the service being offered.

    How can I find work?

    Search through for projects that match your skills. Make sure you post your skills to your profile so people searching for your skills can find you.

    How can I hire people immediately?

    Sign up, post a project for free or browse for the skilled people you want to hire, and Invite them.
